Background

Welcome to my website! Here you will get to know all about my one true passion: data.

My data story began during my time as a waitress. Staring at receipts over and over again got my mind thinking. How much does this restaurant make in sales? How many people contribute to that number? Maybe it’s families, today seems like alot of families, but not yesterday. The questions just kept flowing in. And that's when it happened, I knew exactly what I needed to do.

Data is raw, it’s real, but most importantly it can provide real, objective answers. My goal is to create meaningful data analysis, reports and visualizations and, if needed, provide solutions. Whether it is big data or small data, there is always a story to tell or a trend to investigate, and I am here to help you do that.
