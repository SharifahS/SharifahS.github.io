Project Summary

    An analysis of crimes in Toronto from 2014-2019. The Project was done in Power BI, creating interactive graphs and maps to to convey all crime activity in Toronto.

    Slide 1 - The First slide looks at the Divisions, based on this you can see in some form where most crimes happen.

    Slide 2 - The next slide shows the total of crimes per year, you can see crime volume has increased every year since 2014.

    Slide 3 - This was an interactive map giving you the ability to see crime levels based on area.

    Slide 4 - This slide you the percent of crime type, as well as a breakdown of the percent of crimes commited per month.

    Slide 5 - This slide give you a breakdown of crime by days of the week and type of property where they take place. Here you can see what crimes that are most popular on certain days of the week

    Slide 6 - This is a clip of the a database that would provides all general information of any reported crime in Toronto.

    Slide 7 - The slide is an overal look at the different aspects of offences committed, total, time, week and premise.
